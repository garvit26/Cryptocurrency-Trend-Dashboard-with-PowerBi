# Cryptocurrency-Trend-Dashboard-with-PowerBi


A dynamic, multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize historical trends of major cryptocurrencies. This report provides a high-level overview of key market metrics and allows for a deeper dive into year-wise price action.


---

## üåü Key Features

* **Interactive Slicers:** Filter the entire report by:
    * **Cryptocurrency:** Binance Coin, Bitcoin, Dogecoin, Ethereum, NEM, Stellar, and XRP.
    * **Year:** 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2021.
* **At-a-Glance KPI Cards:**
    * **Volume:** Total trading volume for the selected period.
    * **MarketCap:** Total market capitalization.
    * **High:** The highest price recorded.
    * **Average:** The average price across the period.
    * **Low:** The lowest price recorded.
* **Time-Series Visualizations:**
    * **Marketcap by Date:** A line chart visualizing the historical market capitalization.
    * **High and Low by Date:** A comparative line chart to identify volatility and price boundaries.
    * **Open and Close by Date:** A line chart to analyze daily and long-term price trends.
* **Multi-Page Navigation:** A clean navigation button to switch between the main dashboard and the detailed "Price Trends Analysis" page.
